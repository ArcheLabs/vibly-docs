# Claim VIB

## Overview

In the Incentivized Testnet, eligible participants can claim VIB tokens through the Claim process.

## Claim process

### 1. Payment asset

Users can use DOT or PAS as the payment asset to claim VIB.

### 2. Claim record

Each claim generates an on-chain record containing:

- Claim address
- Claim amount
- Payment asset type
- Timestamp

### 3. Anti-duplication

The system prevents duplicate claims through on-chain records. Each address can only claim once per claim round.

## Important notes

- Claim eligibility is dynamically determined by the network based on participation and rules
- Unused claim quotas do not carry over to the next round
- Claimed VIB must be staked before participating in the network

## Risk disclaimer

- Testnet VIB has no fiat currency value
- Testnet rewards do not represent mainnet commitments
- Network rules may be adjusted based on test results
